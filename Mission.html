Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that children and youth who are new to Calgary/Canada feel welcomed. Families who have arrived recently face several challenges such as language, financial, cultural, and systemic barriers. Which results in the opportunities for there children and youth to be limited.

The Pathways to Belonging program addresses these challenges by providing accessible programming that is designed to specifically cater to the needs of the newcomer children and youth.

This initiative not only offers a safe and welcoming environment but also allows for youth to participate in physical, recreational, and creative activities. We will be offering structured play and guided community engagement to help these children foster a sense of friendships and belonging.

By supporting the holistic development of newcomer chilren and youth, the program strengthens families and promotes inclusive communities. It also will help reduces social marginalization and would allow young newcomers to become confident, connected, and empowered

The Programs Key Outcomes the Project Aims to Achieve

Skill Development and Capacity Building

Workshops and organized activities will be used to help build communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills. Moreover, taking advantage of academic and social opportunities, which will supporting long-term success.

Enhanced Social Integration and Sense of Belonging

Structured opportunities will be used to explore the community and interact with peers, allowing participants to build relationships and feel more connected. This will allow for a reduction in isolation and promotes mutual understanding with the community.
